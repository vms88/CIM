Here's a fun, easy way to make a lip scrub using household products! A customizeable DIY project that is useful in every day life! Cater the recipe and steps to best fit your interests. Go ahead and add any essential oils to add to the benefits of this natural lip scrub.

Save money and have fun creating many different variations of this versatile recipe

DIY Instructions

Materials:Honey & Sugar, container for your very own lipscrub (Add any other oils as desired to enhance scrub)

    • Pour 1 teaspoon of honey
    • Pour 2 teaspoons of sugar, preferrably brown sugar
    • Mix together and repeat ratio until satisfied with amount
    • Place into personalized container
    • Clean off any excess and make sure it fits nicely in container
    • Enjoy!!
