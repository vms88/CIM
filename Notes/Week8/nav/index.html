About

Bulldogs are a docile,willfull, and friendly breed of dog. They have origins in the United Kingdom. For example, a bulldog maybe classified as British or English Bulldog, unless otherwise specified. There are also French and American Bulldogs.
They are popular choices for pets since they are calm dogs. They have a life expectancy of 8-10 years. Bulldogs have very distinct features. They are thick set dogs and have smushed faces with loose skin. While there are many different categories of Bulldogs it is very hard to mistake another dog for a bulldog.
The gait of a bulldog is very distinct. They can even weigh up to 50 pounds. While they are not in the sport group of dogs they still participate in moderate exercise. Their build ensures that they must be moderately active to maintain their health.
